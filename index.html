Strategy & Vision

The State of Qatar is moving with great resolve and determination on the road of development and prosperity under the wise leadership of His Highness the Emir, Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani. It is managing and utilizing its hydrocarbon resources to build a modern infrastructure and a sustainable knowledge-based economy.
