About Us

Who Are We ?

Al Dana Investment and Real Estate Development is an Oman based company established in 2021 with also a branch in Qatar. We deal with Real Estate investors from around the world and have very good business relationship with Europe and Far East.AL-Dana has grown into one of the Middle East's most compelling and prosperous real estate developers. We've created some of the most incredible projects in history, winning praise on a global scale. Al Dana administration is in OMAN, and our work has an even wider influence. We have extensive experience constructing high-rise buildings, including apartment buildings, office towers, gated communities, and hospitality projects. Since the first day we started creating our vision in 2021, we have never stopped expanding. We've been able to provide our consumers with next-level happiness thanks to our high standards, amazing team, and unwavering commitment to quality. This has helped us establish and maintain a reputation for excellence in the market.
